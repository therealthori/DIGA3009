About SELAH

Pause, Stop & Listen

Meet the Creator

Thoriso Shomang

Developer & Believer

Hi! I'm Thoriso Shomang, the creator of SELAH. This project was born out of my own need for a dedicated digital space to nurture my relationship with God.

In our fast-paced, always-connected world, I found it increasingly difficult to carve out intentional time for devotion, prayer, and spiritual reflection. I wanted to create something that would help me — and others — pause, be still, and connect with God in a meaningful way.

SELAH combines my passion for faith and technology, bringing together Scripture, worship music, and journaling into one peaceful sanctuary. Whether you're starting your day with a devotional, spending time in prayer, or reflecting on God's word through journaling, my hope is that SELAH becomes a blessed tool in your spiritual journey.

"Be still, and know that I am God." - Psalm 46:10

My Mission

To provide a peaceful, accessible online sanctuary where believers can:

  • Engage with daily devotional content
  • Spend intentional time in prayer with worship music
  • Journal their spiritual journey and reflect on Scripture
  • Grow deeper in their relationship with God
