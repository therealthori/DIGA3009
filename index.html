SELAH

Pause, Stop & Listen

What is SELAH?

SELAH is a Hebrew word found throughout the Psalms, often interpreted as a musical notation meaning "pause" or "stop and listen." It's an invitation to pause in the midst of worship, to reflect, and to meditate on what has been said.

This website embodies that same spirit — a digital space where you can pause from the chaos of daily life, stop to connect with God, and listen to His voice through Scripture, prayer, and reflection.

Start each day with scripture and thoughtful commentary. Generate new devotions or save your favorites for later reflection.

Time for Prayer

Set aside dedicated time for prayer with our timer feature, accompanied by worship music to create a peaceful atmosphere.

Journal for Care

Read through the Psalms and record your reflections. Mark favorite passages and save prayers for future reference.
